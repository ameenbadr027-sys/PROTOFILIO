About Me

I am Ehab Amin Mohamed, a Computer Science graduate from Zagazig University with a Very Good (High) overall grade.

My journey began in Embedded Systems, where I developed several hardware-based projects using Proteus. Later, I moved into IoT development using ESP32-CAM and built a Smart Glasses project capable of detecting objects and storing captured images inside a database.

I also worked as a Backend Developer using PHP. Recently, I transitioned into Data Analysis, where I built a Heart Disease Prediction project and strengthened my analytical and business intelligence skills.

Throughout my university journey, I actively participated in IEEE, GDG, Zag Eng, and served as a Core Team Member at 3Loom Team.
